Welcome to Momentum

Small steps fuel unstoppable progress—keep your habits in motion.

Momentum is a habit tracker app designed to help users build and maintain positive routines. With an intuitive interface and seamless progress tracking, Momentum keeps users motivated by providing visual streaks, reminders, and personalized insights.

a 1-on-1 accountability partner is someone you’re paired with to help you stay on track with your habits. They check in with you regularly, offer encouragement, and make sure you’re staying consistent. This personalized support helps keep you motivated and accountable, making it easier to stick with your goals and stay committed throughout your journey.

the statistics feature tracks your habit streaks and visualizes your progress through graphs. It helps you see how consistent you've been over time, making it easier to stay motivated. The graphs provide a clear view of your achievements, showing trends and patterns in your habits, so you can celebrate milestones and identify areas for improvement.
